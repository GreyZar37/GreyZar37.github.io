About Me

I am a 21-year-old originally from Lithuania and now residing in Denmark. My passion lies in programming, design, and the development of various virtual products. I delved into programming at the age of 15, with my first language being C#. I initially created games utilizing Unity Game Engine and Visual Studio. However, my interests expanded beyond game development, leading me to explore web development using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. I am constantly eager to broaden my knowledge and skills within this industry.
